Team

Bootstrap blog is maintained by a small team of bootstrap and Python enthusiasts We’re actively looking to grow this team and would love to hear from you if you’re excited about all this.

Blog Purpose and history

Originally created by GV that wanted to share his interests and passion about Bootstrap, Python and programming in general, Bootstrap blog in a short notice became one of the most popular computer science blog websites, with many members and followers.
